Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information. All donor data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent. Your medical information is kept strictly confidential and is only accessible to authorized medical personnel.

Eligibility Guidelines

To donate blood, you must be at least 18 years old (16-17 with parent/guardian consent), weigh at least 110 lbs (50 kilograms), and be in good general health. Certain medical conditions, medications, or recent travel may temporarily defer your eligibility. A health screening is conducted before each donation to ensure your safety and the safety of blood recipients.

Data Retention Policy

Donor records are maintained for a minimum of 10 years as required by health regulations. You may request access to your personal data or request deletion of non-essential information at any time. Donation history is retained to ensure proper tracking and to maintain blood supply safety.

Terms of Service

By registering as a donor, you agree to provide accurate information and follow all donation guidelines. You understand that blood donation is voluntary and you may withdraw consent at any time. RedTrace reserves the right to defer donors who do not meet eligibility criteria.

Safety Standards

All blood collection follows strict FDA guidelines and AABB standards. Equipment is sterile and single-use. Our staff are trained professionals who prioritize donor and recipient safety. All donated blood undergoes rigorous testing before distribution.

Non-Discrimination Policy

RedTrace welcomes all eligible donors regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or background. Eligibility is determined solely by FDA guidelines and medical criteria. We are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all donors.
